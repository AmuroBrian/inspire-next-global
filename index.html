CONGLOMERATE MODEL
The foundational philosophy of the Conglomerate model revolves around placing capital or the financial institution at the epicenter of its operations. Under this model, subsidiary and affiliated companies collaborate with external investors, political allies and affiliates, all united by a common objective: fostering capital expansion and augmenting their sphere of influence.
In essence, the Conglomerate model is rooted in the strategic alignment of financial resources and relationships. It thrives on the synergy between a central financial entity and a network of associated businesses, all orchestrated to maximize wealth accumulation and wield substantial influence in both economic and political arenas. This intricate web of connections and interests amplifies the collective capacity for growth and leverage, making the Conglomerate model a formidable force in the business world.

ORGANIZATION CHART

The organizational structure of Inspire Next Global Inc. follows a clear hierarchical format. At the top is the President, who oversees all operations and departments. Directly reporting to the President are the Assistant President, Legal Executive, and two Non-Executive Members who provide support and advisory roles. The company is divided into three main departments: Administration, Marketing, and Technical Operations. The Administration Department is led by the OIC/Admin, supported by an OIC, a Technical Administrator who manages IT systems, and a Loan Officer who handles loan-related tasks. The Marketing Department is headed by two Marketing Presidents, supported by two Marketing Vice Presidents. Reporting to them is the Marketing Team, composed of three team members responsible for executing marketing activities and campaigns. This structure promotes clear leadership, streamlined communication, and efficient workflow across departments, ensuring effective coordination and productivity throughout the organization.

PRIVACY POLICY

Inspire Next Global Inc. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") establishes the following privacy policy, builds a framework for the protection of personal information, and promotes the protection of personal information by ensuring that all employees recognize the importance of personal information protection and engage in related efforts.

  1. Purpose of Using Personal Information
    2. Personal information entrusted by customers will be used for communication from the Company, business guidance, responding to inquiries, delivering newsletters related to services provided on our website, authentication in the respective services, payment transactions, and statistical processing in a form that does not identify individuals.
  2. Security Management of Personal Information
    3. To prevent unauthorized access, loss, damage, alteration, leakage, etc., the Company will take necessary security measures, such as maintaining and managing security systems, establishing a management structure, and thoroughly educating employees on security. When handling personal information abroad, the Company will promptly understand the personal information protection system of the relevant country, take appropriate security measures, and provide information to customers.
  3. Non-Disclosure and Non-Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties
    4. The Company will appropriately manage personal information entrusted by customers and will not disclose or provide it to third parties except in the following cases:
    1. When there is customer consent
    2. When disclosure is necessary for providing the desired service to customers through outsourcing business to subcontractors
    3. When providing personal information through joint use
    4. When personal information is provided due to business succession
    5. When disclosure is based on laws and regulations
  4. Outsourcing
    5. To provide better services to customers, the Company may outsource business related to the handling of personal information as needed. In such cases, the Company will evaluate and select outsourcing partners appropriately and supervise them adequately.
  5. Joint Use
    6. The Company may jointly use personal data as follows:
    1. Items of personal information to be jointly used: Name, address, email address, phone number, gender, age, date of birth, and all information provided by customers to the Company
    2. Purpose of using jointly used personal data: As stated in item 1
    3. Manager responsible for joint use: The Company (Inspire Next Global Inc., 26th High Street Corporate Plaza, Bonifacio High Street, BGC Taguig City)
  6. Disclosure, Correction, Deletion, etc. of Personal Information
    7. If a customer wishes to disclose, correct, add, delete, suspend the use, or suspend the provision to third parties of their personal information, they can make such a request following the Company's prescribed procedures. The Company will respond after confirming the customer's identity and in accordance with applicable laws. Note that if a customer requests the disclosure of personal information or third-party provision records, the Company may charge a disclosure fee in accordance with the prescribed procedures.
  7. Compliance with Laws and Review of Privacy Policy
    8. The Company complies with applicable laws (including foreign laws) and related guidelines regarding the handling of personal information. The Company will review the contents of the privacy policy as needed and strive for improvement. If there are changes to the privacy policy, the revised policy will be posted on the Company's website for confirmation.
